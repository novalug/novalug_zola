Participation in NoVaLUG is free and open to all.

The best way to participate is to **attend our meetings**. You can view all meeting information, include calendar subscription information, on our [meetings page](/meetings).

This page describes how to participate in NoVaLUG online. Please read our [Code of Conduct](/participate/code-of-conduct).
